TRIENDS

A location-based social service app that builds relationship between people have similar daily routine and interests

Needs Finding

Interviews

User interviews were conducted to understand the target group. Daily life: What do they do in their life. How is their daily routine like. What do they do in their free time. Relationships: What kind of live they expect in a relationship. What methods have you used to find the one. Was there any other people try to help you, and how?

Questionnaires

Questionnaires were conducted based on the result of interviews to get statistic data of the insights of common in the target group

“I think I just haven’t met the right one. But life is so tense that I don’t have time to join activities to meet new people.”

“It takes time to know a person, and after took some time to learn each other, we realized that we were not suitable for each other.”

“I've met someone I’m interested in who is my friend’s friend and added him in my social network later. But it’s difficult to get to know him further."

Persona

Andrew | 25 | Ph.D Student | Single

Andrew is eager to have a relationship. But he has limited social circle and few opportunity to know new people. Also, he is lack of dating experience.

Developing

I used web languages to develop the app, since it's more easier to test on computer end and can be easily migrated to different mobile OSs. Angular.js is used as the MVC framework. Weather Underground and Google Map APIs are used to get weather and map contents.

Final Design

LIFE TRACK

Triends will record user’s life track, user can review their life track at anytime, take notes and read analyse, realize some fact they even’t know about themselves. The track data is used to match friends who have the similar life track as users, while take of user’s privacy carefully.

DISCOVER

Events is the connection between track and friends. User set their preferred event category and get notification, put the event want to go into their Life Track, and invite friends.

FRIENDS

Triends will match and suggest friends for users according to user’s accumulated data, such as life track, where they often go, what course they take, the type of events they like, etc. Beyond basic information, users can see the common things they share with the suggested friend.
