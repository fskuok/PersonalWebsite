THE RULE IN A NUTSHELL

Design and build an one-meter “4-floors-building” using bamboo slice. After putting on loads, the structure will endure 1 minute simulated 8.0 Lithe magnitude earthquake 3 times. The lighter the structure is and the heavier loads it bears, the better

MAKING THE MODEL

The structure is all hand-made with 0.3mm bamboo slice.
