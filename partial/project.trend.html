LIEF TRACK

Trend will record user’s life track, user can review their life track at anytime, take notes and read analyse, realize some fact they even’t know about themself. The track data is used to match friends who have the similar life track as users, while take of user’s privacy carefully.

DISCOVER

Events is the connection between track and friends. User set their prefered event category and get notification, put the event want to go into their Life Track, and invite friends.

FRIENDS

Trend will match and suggest friends for users according to user’s accumulated data, such as life track, where they often go, what course they take, the type of events they like, etc. Beyond basic informations, users can see the common things they share with the suggested friend.
