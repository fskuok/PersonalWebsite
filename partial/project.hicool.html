Design

1.Organized & Intuitive

Organize things with intuitive categories, color and icons

Process 1: Researched on most common hiking first-aid situations and put categorized medicines and supplies accordingly.

Process 2: Put those first-aid situations in three categories, assigned represent color

Process 3: Made icons for easier searching, added a geographic specified page

Common hiking first-aid situations:
Froze bite | Scratches | Wrench | Sprain |
Broken bones | Severe bleeding | Burn | Cuts |
Allergy | Stings | Spider bite | Snake bite |
Head | Stomach | Heart

2. Easy-accessible Guide

Beyond hard copy manual, users can scan QR Code for offline first-aid guide in Hicool App, which is especially useful in low-light environment

3. Easy-accessible Medicines

We designed packages of medicines can be easily opened and took out by one hand.

4. Customizable & Replaceable

Contents are separated in module pages; Beyond normal contents, we provide hikers geographic location specific first-aid modules for their destinations
