This is a story... about me and Bob...

Bob lives in the same apartment building with me. Bob is that kind of person that likes adventure and never satisfy.

One day, I came across him in the lobby, and he said...

Hey, do you know birdhouses? I find them interesting, but I think that's for house livers, they have trees in yard to install it. Anyway, it will be interesting if I can have one and observe the birds in my birdhouse.

I know a kind of birdhouse, you can fix it outside of your window, then you can watch them through the transparent back surface. You can buy it on Amazon, why don't you try it?

One week passed, I came across Bob in the lobby, again...

I've tried that. It worked at the beginning, but birds can see me inside house, they will not come back again. Also, It blocks the view of my window... Oh, you are a designer, right? why don't you design one for me?

So I designed a birdhouse and showed it to Bob

There is a mirror inside the birdhouse that can reflect the top view of the birdhouse. Also, it's not easy for birds to discover, and most of it's volume is below the window sill so it will not block too much of the window.

That's nice. But ummmm..., it's a little bit of boring

After a while, I showed Bob my new design

An flower pot is designed which can be stacked on the birdhouse. Planting bird-attracting plants in it can increase the possibility of bird using the birdhouse. Moreover, it's a decoration for the window. The flower pot can be used standalone, without the birdhouse, buying them as accessories and change the flower above the birdhouse may attract different kinds of bird.

That's nice. But ummmm..., it's still not cool enough. Can you add some features such as playing music, something like an iPod?

Sure, I've already prepared it for you...
